# AI Video Prompt Quality Rubric

Apply this rubric after designing the visual idea and before returning an AI-video prompt.

## Required Checks

Score each item as pass or revise:

1. **Context fit**
   - The visual reflects the surrounding argument, not an isolated keyword.
   - It does not introduce an unsupported claim.
2. **Communication value**
   - The viewer can understand why this visual accompanies the sentence.
   - It adds explanation, emotion, interest, evidence, contrast, or pacing value.
3. **Visible action**
   - The clip has a clear starting state, primary action, and readable result.
   - Abstract meaning has been converted into something visible.
4. **Protagonist purpose**
   - If the AI animated protagonist appears, performance adds more value than a non-character visual.
   - Role, expression, body language, and continuity are specified.
5. **Generation feasibility**
   - One stable location, one primary action, limited cast, and controlled camera movement.
   - No dependence on accurate generated text, exact logos, or complex choreography.
6. **Prompt specificity**
   - Subject, scene, props, action, composition, camera, lighting, style, ratio, and duration are clear.
7. **Editorial usefulness**
   - The generated clip can be trimmed into the recommended placement without losing its meaning.

Revise any prompt that fails one or more checks.

## Bad-to-Better Pattern

Weak:

`一个男人拒绝很多事情，电影感，1:1。`

Why weak:

- Literal and generic.
- No contextual role, action arc, or controllable camera.
- Does not show whether refusal is strategic, fearful, or careless.

Better:

`固定形象的 AI 动画主人公扮演正在进行战略取舍的创业负责人，坐在简洁会议室主位，桌面摊开五份不同项目提案。起初他快速翻看所有文件并略显犹豫，随后神情坚定地将其中四份依次推到桌侧，只把一份关键提案留在正中央，双手压住文件并抬头做出决定。中景侧前方构图，镜头缓慢推进至唯一保留的提案，冷静专业的自然光，写实动画质感，方形画面 1:1，8 秒。避免可读文字、避免多人抢镜、避免多余手指。`
